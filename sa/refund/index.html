Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Summary: Due to the immediate-download nature of our digital products, all sales are final and non-refundable after purchase — except in the case of a documented technical defect in the PDF file itself.

1. No-Refund Policy for Digital Downloads

Because our products are digital files that are delivered instantly upon payment, and because the customer gains immediate access to the full content, we do not offer refunds or exchanges once a purchase is completed and the download link has been delivered.

This policy is consistent with standard digital goods regulations and is clearly disclosed prior to purchase.

2. Exception: Technical Defects

We will issue a full refund or a replacement file if:

  • The PDF file is corrupt or unreadable upon download.
  • The file content does not match the product description on our website.
  • You did not receive your download link within 24 hours of payment confirmation.

To qualify, you must contact us within 7 days of purchase and provide documentation of the technical issue (e.g., screenshot of the error).

3. How to Request a Refund

Email support@smartpdf.shop with:

  • Your order number or receipt from Paddle.
  • A description and screenshot of the issue.

We will respond within 2 business days and, if eligible, process your refund through Paddle within 5–10 business days.

4. Contact

Refund inquiries: support@smartpdf.shop
