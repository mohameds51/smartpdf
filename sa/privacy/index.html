Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

SmartPDF.shop ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit smartpdf.shop/sa.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, email address, payment details during checkout) and automatically (IP address, browser type, pages visited) through standard analytics tools and our payment processor Paddle.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your purchase and deliver your digital product.
  • To send you your order confirmation and download link via email.
  • To improve our website and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely by Paddle.com. We do not store your credit card or Apple Pay details on our servers. Paddle acts as the Merchant of Record and handles all payment data in compliance with PCI DSS standards.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and may use analytics cookies (e.g., Facebook Pixel) to measure advertising performance. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with Paddle (payment processing) and Meta (advertising analytics via Facebook Pixel), both operating under their own privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email and order data for up to 3 years for legal and accounting purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@smartpdf.shop to exercise these rights.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email us at support@smartpdf.shop.
